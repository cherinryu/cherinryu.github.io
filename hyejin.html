♡ & ∞ in †

Everyone who thinks and builds up their own unique path deserves to be considered an artist. We all live in different manners; but, as long as we discover and challenge something new in life, we are all 'artists'. 

Born in Korea, raised in Guatemala, and temporarily living in NJ/NYC. Hyejin Lee is a student at Fashion Institute of Technology. She focuses on 'discovering' rather than creating, which allows her to admit that art is a connection between expression and inspired ideas not a proud masterpiece or creation of an individual.
